Privacy Policy — The Overland Trail

Last updated: July 23, 2026

The Overland Trail ("the App") is a single-player game. This policy explains what information the App does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress (party status, supplies, mileage, etc.) is kept only in the device's local memory while the App is open and is not saved to any server or third party.

Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device permissions beyond what is required to display the game on screen.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or any third-party tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because the App collects no personal information from any user, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same address.
